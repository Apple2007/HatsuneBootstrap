Recommendations for a New VOCALOID/Miku Fan:

So, you want to become a new VOCALOID and/or Hatsune Miku fan? Well, you've come to the right place! Here are some recommendations for you when it comes to music, fan communities, YouTube videos, and more!


Music Recommendations:

Starting with music recommendations, there are a couple of artists I would start out with, since they have made some of the most influential/most popular VOCALOID songs ever to date:


  • kz-livetune: Known for songs such as:
    • Tell Your World
    • Yellow
    • Catch The Wave
  • wowaka: Known for songs such as:
    • Rolling Girl
    • Unknown Mother Goose
    • Unhappy Refrain
  • DECO*27: Known for songs such as:
    • Rabbit Hole
    • Ghost Rule
    • The Vampire
  • ryo (Supercell): Known for songs such as:
    • World is Mine
    • Melt
    • Love Me Gimme

Tell Your World by kz-livetune

Rolling Girl by wowaka

Ghost Rule by DECO*27

World is Mine by ryo (Supercell)
